Explore how the Artificial Neural Network (ANN) predicts high achievers based on math, reading, and writing scores.

What is Artificial Neural Network (ANN)?

Artificial Neural Networks are advanced machine learning models inspired by brain neural structures, capable of capturing complex patterns in data.

In this project, ANN is trained to classify whether a student is a high achiever based on their performance in key academic areas.

What I Did & How I Did It

Using the StudentsPerformance_with_high_achiever dataset, the model was trained on three primary features:

Math Score – Student’s score in math exams (0-100).
Reading Score – Student’s score in reading assessments (0-100).
Writing Score – Student’s writing proficiency score (0-100).

Model Configuration:

TARGET_COL = "high_achiever"
FEATURES = ["math score", "reading score", "writing score"]

The ANN was optimized using training and validation datasets, minimizing loss to improve prediction accuracy.
